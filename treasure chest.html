Treasure Chest Project

Ciara was hired as the studio set designer in Design Works photography studio. She designed and fabricated props for their new themed photoshoot “Mermaids and Pirates”. The treasure chest was one of these props.

Prop Making Step by Step

  1. Materials sourced
  2. Wood measured and cut
  3. Wood sanded and organised
  4. Main body glued and drilled
  5. Chest top nailed and glued
  6. Chest sanded smooth and cleaned
  7. Chest primed and painted
  8. Chest sanded to give weathered look
  9. Chest repainted
  10. Chest decoration added
  11. Decoration painted and chest touched up
